Overview
The Limits to Growth study conducted on the terrestrial exo-3; models humanity’s consumption, growth and expansion on this potential new world and provides solutions to ensure that our collective past mistreatment of earth is not repeated.
The Project on the Predicament of Mankind – The intent of the project is to examine the complex of problems troubling men of all nations: poverty in the midst of plenty; degradation of the environment; loss of faith in institutions; uncontrolled urban spread; insecurity of employment; alienation of youth; rejection of traditional values; and inflation and other monetary and economic disruptions. These seemingly divergent parts make up the “World Problematique”.

MISSION
Trident is NASA’s first campaign in accurately assessing a potential terrestrial for recolonisation to establish accurate imaging and data to model potential terrestrial usage, potential production, resource depletion, population growth and pollution management. The proposed world3 model uses neural networks and birth death-cycles to accurate depict human growth and consumption on a new potential host world and help define how best to create a new system of life.
With these limits to growth, we hope that it will command critical attention and spark debate in all societies about how we can better use this new home.
