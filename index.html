ABOUT
Discovery of terrestrial exoplanets in a vital part of space exploration and the potential future growth of the human species as its unparalleled adventure into the galactic stars. The goals of these expeditions into the void is to identify suitable liveable planets with abundant resources, land availability for population growth and low pollution that could be used as a second home for humanity as all resources have been extinguished from our original source world.
These images, data, experiments and analysis projects will help establish if this exoplanet has the correct ratio life for sustained human growth into the year 2100, and what will be needed on this expedition into the stars. The world3 model will be best used to simulate the liveability and projected exponential growth of continual human life on discovered exoplanets.
